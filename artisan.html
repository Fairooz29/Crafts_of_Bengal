About Rina

Rina Begum is a renowned potter from Rajshahi, Bangladesh, with over 20 years of experience in the craft. She learned pottery from her family and has since mastered the art, creating exquisite pieces that reflect the rich cultural heritage of her region. Her work is characterized by intricate designs and traditional techniques, making each piece a unique work of art. Rina's dedication to preserving and promoting traditional pottery has earned her recognition both locally and internationally.
